How It Works

Simple steps to manage your institution effectively

1

Create Your Account

Register as an administrator to get access to the full management system. Your account will have full privileges to manage all aspects of the system.

2

Add Students and Courses

Begin by adding your student body and course offerings to the system. You can import data in bulk or add records individually.

3

Manage Enrollments

Enroll students in courses, track capacities, and manage waiting lists. The system automatically prevents over-enrollment.

4

Record Grades and Progress

As the semester progresses, record grades and track student performance. Generate progress reports as needed.

5

Generate Reports

At any point, generate comprehensive reports on student performance, course statistics, and institutional data.

About EduManage

Our mission is to simplify educational administration through technology.

EduManage was developed to address the challenges faced by educational institutions in managing student data, course offerings, and enrollment processes. Our system provides a comprehensive solution that saves time, reduces errors, and provides valuable insights through reporting.

The platform was built using modern web technologies including Flask for the backend, PostgreSQL for data storage, and Bootstrap for the responsive interface. It incorporates advanced database features like stored procedures, triggers, and complex queries to ensure data integrity and performance.
